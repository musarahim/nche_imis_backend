National Council for Higher Education is pleased to announce a call for papers for presentation in the 7th Annual Higher Education Conference, the 3rd NCHE Regional Conference to be held in Gulu City.

Theme: Harnessing Innovation in Higher Education for Accelerated Economic Transformation.

The successful submissions vetted through our criteria will be published in the forthcoming edition of the Uganda Higher Education Review Journal, Volume 13, Issue 2, 2026. The journal holds ISSN 1813-2243 (in print), 2958-5473 (electronic), and a DOI 10.58653.

Research areas of focus in this volume .13, issue 2, 2026 include the following;

  1. Adoption of Comptence-Based Curricula and Innovative Pedagogical Reforms for the transformation of Higher Education.
  2. Fostering Innovations, Entrepreneurship, and Start-Up Ecosystems in Higher Education.
  3. Strengthening University-Industry Linkages and Knowledge Transfer.
  4. Transformative Research and Innovation in STEM for National Research and Development.

Abstract and Paper Submission

All papers submitted will be peer-reviewed and evaluated based on originality, technical and/or research depth, accuracy and relevance to the focus areas

  1. Submitted abstracts to be evaluated by the Journal editorial Committee.

  3. All submissions should report original and previously unpublished research results no matter the type of research paper you are presenting.
