16th Annual Higher Education Exhibition

26th to 28th March 2026 | Gulu University, Gulu City

The National Council for Higher Education (NCHE) is mandated under Section 5(b) of the Universities and Other Tertiary Institutions Act, 2001 (as amended), to promote and develop the processing and dissemination of information on higher education for the benefit of the public.

In fulfillment of this mandate, NCHE annually organizes the Higher Education Exhibition, a national platform for institutions of higher learning to showcase innovations, academic programs, research, and contributions to national development. The 16th Annual Higher Education Exhibition provides a unique opportunity to highlight the role of innovation in transforming education and driving economic development.

Theme: Harnessing Innovation in Higher Education for Accelerated Economic Transformation.

The 2026 theme emphasizes the pivotal role of innovation in transforming Uganda’s economy through research, technology, and skills development within higher education institutions. It aims to foster partnerships, knowledge sharing, and strategic initiatives to promote sustainable economic growth.

Objectives

  • To showcase innovations and best practices from higher education institutions in Uganda and the region.
  • To promote collaboration between academia, industry, and government for national development.
  • To provide a platform for prospective students and the public to interact with institutions and learn about academic programs.
  • To foster discussions on the role of higher education in achieving Uganda’s Vision 2040 and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • To encourage the integration of technology and innovation in education and institutional management.

Why Participate?

  • Provides a national platform to showcase research, technology, and innovations.
  • Informs the public about programs, career options, and institutional offerings.
  • Enhances visibility of institutions across the country and region.
  • Facilitates collaboration between institutions, industry, and government.
  • Helps institutions attract prospective students.
  • Offers career guidance and mentorship for students.
  • Provides recognition for excellence and creativity.
